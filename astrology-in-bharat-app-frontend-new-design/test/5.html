What is Mangal Dosha?

Manglik Dosh is caused when Mars occupies the 1st, 4th, 7th, 8th or 12th house in a native's birth chart. It signifies energy and passion.

Effects of Mangal Dosha

In Vedic Astrology, Mars represents drive, ego, self-esteem, and energy. When Mars is placed malefic houses, it creates Mangal Dosha. This condition is believed to cause disharmony in married life.

However, not every Mangal Dosha is harmful. There are various cancellations (Mangal Dosha Bhanga) that occur based on planetary positions. Our advanced calculator checks for these exceptions before giving a verdict.
