The Significance of the Moon Sign

In Vedic Astrology, the Moon (Chandra) is considered the most significant planet because it represents the mind (Manas). While the Sun represents the soul, the Moon governs our emotions, temperament, and how we react to situations instinctively. Your Moon sign is the zodiac sign where the Moon was positioned at the exact time of your birth.

It determines your "Rashi" and plays a pivotal role in Kundli matching, daily horoscope predictions, and understanding your compatibility with others. A strong Moon brings mental peace and emotional stability, while a weak Moon can cause anxiety and mood fluctuations.

Positive Traits

  • Intuitive and empathetic nature
  • Strong emotional intelligence
  • Nurturing and protective of loved ones
  • Creative and imaginative mind

Challenges

  • Mood swings and unpredictability
  • Over-sensitivity to criticism
  • Tendency to hold onto past grudges
  • Can become overly dependent emotionally
