Why Check Nakshatra Compatibility?

In Vedic Astrology, Nakshatras (lunar mansions) play a crucial role in determining the mental and emotional compatibility between two individuals. While Rashi (Moon Sign) matching is popular, Nakshatra Matching goes deeper into the subconscious nature.

Our Nakshatra Milan tool evaluates the Nadi Dosha, Bhakoot Dosha, and Gana Dosha among others, providing a comprehensive score out of 36 points (Gunas).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good Nakshatra Matching score? expand_more
A score of 18 or above out of 36 is generally considered acceptable for marriage. Scores above 25 are considered excellent.
Can Nakshatra Milan predict divorce? expand_more
It indicates compatibility and potential friction areas. While a low score suggests challenges, divorce prediction requires a detailed analysis of individual horoscopes (Kundalis) by an expert.
Is Nadi Dosha serious? expand_more
Nadi Dosha is considered a significant flaw in Vedic Astrology, often related to health and progeny issues. However, there are many exceptions and cancellations (Parihar) which an astrologer can check.
