Aries Overview

Aries are people of action. There is no stopping them once they have set their mind on a goal. They will achieve it by hook or by crook. They are best when they are leading the pack. They do not like to be followers. They are independent and like to do things their own way.

In relationships, Aries are passionate and adventurous. They love the thrill of the chase. They can be very romantic, but they also need their space. They are compatible with other Fire signs, Leo and Sagittarius, as well as Air signs, Gemini and Aquarius.
