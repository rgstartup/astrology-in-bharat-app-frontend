Understanding the 36 Gunas

In Vedic Astrology, Kundali Matching or Horoscope Matching is vital for marriage. The process involves matching 8 distinct aspects (Ashtakoota) of the bride and groom's personality.

Each aspect carries points (Gunas), totaling 36. A score of 18 or above is considered acceptable for marriage, while a score above 24 is considered excellent.

  • check_circle Varna (Work Compatibility) - 1 Point
  • check_circle Vashya (Dominance) - 2 Points
  • check_circle Tara (Destiny) - 3 Points
  • check_circle Yoni (Mental Compatibility) - 4 Points
